# NLA.ae Design Analysis

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: Deep burgundy/maroon (#8B1538 or similar)
- **Background**: Clean white/off-white
- **Text**: Dark charcoal/black for body text
- **Accents**: Gold/copper for highlights
- **Neutral**: Light gray for borders and backgrounds

## Typography
- **Clean, editorial serif or sans-serif**
- Large, generous spacing between sections
- Clear hierarchy with large headings
- Arabic and English fonts balanced
- Professional, institutional feel

## Layout Principles
- **Generous whitespace** - breathing room between all elements
- **Full-width hero** with curved bottom edge (wave shape)
- **Grid-based sections** with consistent padding
- **Icon-based service cards** with simple line icons
- **Horizontal scrolling carousels** for content collections
- **Subtle shadows** on cards (not heavy drop shadows)
- **Thin borders** (1px) where needed

## Navigation
- **Top utility bar** with language switcher, login, cart
- **Main navigation** below with clear labels
- **Dropdown menus** with organized sections
- **Sticky header** on scroll
- **Clean, minimal design**

## Card Design
- **Simple white cards** with subtle shadow
- **Icon or image at top**
- **Title and brief description**
- **Minimal decoration** - no gradients or bright colors
- **Hover states** with subtle elevation change
- **Consistent padding** and spacing

## Image Treatment
- **Full-width hero images** with text overlay
- **Curved/wave dividers** between sections
- **Grayscale or muted historical photos**
- **No bright, saturated colors**
- **Professional photography style**

## Key Takeaways for Freej AlMarar
1. Use **sand/beige/off-white** as base with **deep burgundy or copper** accent
2. Remove all rainbow gradients and bright colors
3. Apply **generous whitespace** and clean typography
4. Use **simple card designs** with subtle shadows
5. Implement **curved section dividers** for visual interest
6. Keep **institutional, museum-quality** aesthetic throughout
7. Use **line icons** instead of filled/colorful icons
8. Apply **subtle hover effects** (elevation, not color changes)


## Additional Observations

### Archive Collection Display
- **Grid layout** with historical photos
- **Sepia/grayscale tones** for historical authenticity
- **Equal-sized thumbnails** in clean grid
- **Minimal borders** between items
- **Hover effects** show subtle overlay

### News/Events Section
- **Card-based layout** with image + text
- **Clean white cards** with subtle shadow
- **Date stamps** in consistent format
- **Brief excerpts** with "Read more" links
- **Consistent spacing** between cards

### Section Headers
- **Large, bold Arabic typography**
- **Centered or right-aligned** (RTL)
- **Ample spacing** above and below
- **No decorative elements** - just clean text

### Color Usage
- **Minimal color** - mostly neutral tones
- **Burgundy/maroon** used sparingly for CTAs and accents
- **Gold** used for special elements (logos, badges)
- **No bright colors** anywhere on the page

### Overall Impression
- **Calm, dignified, institutional**
- **Professional museum quality**
- **Historical authenticity** through muted tones
- **Clean, modern layout** with traditional respect
- **Generous whitespace** creates breathing room
- **Subtle animations** on hover only
